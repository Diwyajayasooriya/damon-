Damon Salvatore

Damon Salvatore is one of the two main male protagonists of The Vampire Diaries. Damon was a 178-year-old vampire and a distant descendant of Silas.

Damon Salvatore Was Stuck at 25 For 178 Years He died with Katherine's blood in his system on September 25, 1864, which was how he transformed into a vampire. His brother, Stefan, encouraged him to complete the transition by feeding on human blood, which was a grudge that Damon held against him for most of their life.

Damon's brother

Stefan is the younger brother of Damon. The Salvatore brothers, Stefan and Damon, were human. They were very close and were best friends in life. Damon always confided and trusted in Stefan and they always defended each other, especially in front of their father.

  • In 1864, Stefan was turned into a vampire after being shot by his father, Giuseppe, with Katherine's blood in his system, before accidentally killing his father. Damon, who also turned into a vampire, promised Stefan an eternity of misery, fueled by his anger and jealousy.
  • In their plan to kill Klaus, Stefan seemingly betrays Damon by saving Klaus, but it's revealed that Stefan actually did it to save Damon's life.
  • Stefan is shot by wooden bullets and Damon saves him.

Damon's girlfriend

Damon gradually involved himself in the lives of many people in Mystic Falls by developing friendships with several humans. After spending time with Elena Gilbert, Damon becomes more empathetic and falls deeply, madly and passionately in love with her. He always puts her safety first before anyone else, even his.

  1. However, when Elena got into a car accident, it was Damon who saved her and behaved well with her. Elena didn't have her vervain necklace with her, and Damon did not try to compel her or mess with her in any way
  2. When "time's up," Elena runs away but while Stefan tries to follow, Klaus makes Stefan "turn it off" and he bites Elena, attempting to drink her blood. Later, Damon saves her from the hospital and brings her to the Salvatore boarding house where she is upset about all that has happened.
